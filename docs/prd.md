# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Personal Portfolio Website – Video Editing → Automation Brand

---

## 1. Product Overview

### Product Name
**Personal Portfolio Website (Brand-First, Client-Facing)**

### Purpose
A high-conversion portfolio website that:
- Positions you as a professional video editor (primary)
- Builds trust with clients and creators
- Gradually evolves into a code automation & systems brand
- Supports long-term transition into an agency

> **This website is not a resume.**  
> It is a sales + credibility + brand authority system.

---

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Users
- YouTubers
- Content creators (Instagram, Shorts, Reels)
- Small brands & startups
- Solo founders

### Secondary Users (Future)
- Founders needing automation
- Agencies seeking automation partners
- SaaS or ops-focused clients

---

## 3. Core Brand Positioning

### Current Position
Video Editor specializing in short-form content, motion graphics, and YouTube systems

### Future Position (Planned Evolution)
Creative + Automation Engineer building scalable content and business systems

### Brand Tone
- Professional
- Confident
- Clear
- Results-focused
- No hype, no fluff

---

## 4. Primary Goals

### Business Goals
- Convert visitors into client inquiries
- Show skill credibility without fake proof
- Clearly explain what you do and who it's for
- Prepare for automation services later without rebranding

### User Goals
- Quickly understand your value
- See your skills visually
- Trust you even if you are early-stage
- Easily contact you

---

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- Portfolio page engagement time
- Contact form submissions
- Direct WhatsApp / email clicks
- Returning visitors
- Google indexing of your name + services

---

## 6. Website Structure (Pages & Sections)

### 6.1 Home Page

#### Hero Section
**Objective:** Instantly communicate value

**Elements:**
- **Headline:**  
  "Video Editing That Helps Creators Grow Faster"
  
- **Subheadline:**  
  Short-form edits, motion graphics, and YouTube systems — built for consistency
  
- **Primary CTA:** "View My Work"
- **Secondary CTA:** "Contact Me"

#### What I Do (Services Snapshot)
- Short-Form Video Editing
- Motion Graphics
- YouTube Video Editing
- Content Systems (Editing + Structure)

#### Why Choose Me
- Focus on clarity, retention, and pacing
- Creator-first mindset
- Systems over random edits
- Clean, modern visuals

#### Future-Ready Note (Soft)
*Small line:*
> Currently focused on video editing. Automation services coming soon.

---

### 6.2 Portfolio / Work Page

**Objective:** Show skills without fake client claims

**Content:**
- Personal edits
- Practice projects
- Re-edited public videos (clearly labeled)
- Motion graphics samples
- Before / after comparisons

**Each Project Includes:**
- Short description
- What skill it shows (pacing, hooks, motion, storytelling)
- Tools used (After Effects, Premiere Pro)

---

### 6.3 Services Page

#### Service 1: Short-Form Video Editing
- Reels, Shorts, TikTok
- Hook-driven editing
- Fast pacing

#### Service 2: Motion Graphics
- Titles
- Animations
- Visual storytelling

#### Service 3: YouTube Editing
- Long-form editing
- Retention-focused structure
- Clean storytelling

#### Pricing Strategy
- "Custom pricing based on project"
- No fixed prices (professional positioning)

---

### 6.4 About Page

**Objective:** Build trust, not biography.

**Content Structure:**
- Who you are (brief)
- What you focus on now
- How you think (systems, consistency, growth)
- Your long-term vision (creative + automation)

**Example tone:**
> I'm building a long-term career around content systems — starting with editing and expanding into automation.

---

### 6.5 Skills & Tools Page (Optional but Powerful)

#### Creative
- After Effects
- Premiere Pro
- Photoshop

#### Automation (Learning / Future-Ready)
- n8n
- Python
- APIs
- Workflow automation

*This shows growth mindset, not over-promising.*

---

### 6.6 Contact Page

**Contact Methods:**
- Simple form (Name, Email, Project Type, Message)
- WhatsApp link
- Email address

**CTA Copy:**
> Let's talk about your content.

## 7. Technical Requirements

### Frontend
- Next.js
- SEO-friendly structure
- Mobile-first design
- Fast load speed

### SEO
- Index your name
- Index "video editor"
- Clean metadata
- Structured headings (H1 → H3)

### Hosting
- Vercel (free)
- No paid domain initially (upgrade later)

---

## 8. Design Requirements

### Visual Style
- Dark or neutral theme
- Minimal
- Strong typography
- Focus on visuals, not text blocks

### UX Principles
- Fewer clicks
- Clear CTAs
- No clutter
- Scroll-friendly
